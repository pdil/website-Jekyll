Coming Spring 2017

Passport allows you to easily check off and categorize the countries you have visited.

Sign up for the public beta.

Features

  • Colorful map to quickly view your countries or share with friends.
  • Create and customize new lists to organize your countries and plan trips.
  • Use location to automatically add new countries to your Passport.
  • iCloud Sync between your iOS devices so your Passport follows you with whatever device you use.
  • Add the states/provinces/regions that you have visited within certain countries (the list is always growing!)
  • Choose from a number of colorful themes.
  • Quick support for any questions or comments that you may have.
Note Some features may required a paid upgrade to Passport Pro ($2.99, or local equivalent).
